Expected Impact (2025-2030)

OSTORG's programmes are expected to lead to the following within the next 2-5 years:

Improved Holistic Development

Enhanced holistic development of vulnerable children and young people across physical, emotional, educational, and social dimensions, leading to well-rounded individuals ready to contribute to society.

Strengthened Partnerships

Community partnerships strengthened to sustain and expand OSTORG activities to the entire Kisii and Nyamira counties, creating a network of support and collaboration.

Talent Development & Empowerment

Children and young people empowered through talent development and training to live a life of dignity and fulfillment for themselves and their families, breaking cycles of poverty and disadvantage.

Empowered Communities

Community empowered to respect, protect, and safeguard vulnerable children and young people, creating a culture of care and responsibility that extends beyond our direct intervention.

Purpose of Partnership & Support

Seeking Partnerships

OSTORG seeks the support of well-wishers and aims to forge partnerships with individuals, government agencies, NGOs, Faith-Based Organizations, and other organizations with similar objectives.

Essential Services

Enable OSTORG to provide children and young people with safe shelter complete with essential services such as:

  • Food and nutrition
  • Medical care
  • Clothing and personal items
  • Protection and security
  • Psycho-social support

Education & Training

Enhance access to:

  • Quality education programs
  • Skills training opportunities
  • Vocational education
  • Talent development initiatives
  • Life skills workshops

Support Services

Facilitate access to:

  • Comprehensive psycho-social support
  • Counseling services
  • Mental health care
  • Trauma healing programs
  • Family reunification support

Advocacy & Protection

Support our efforts to:

  • Foster community partnerships
  • Prevent abuse and exploitation
  • Advocate for children's rights
  • Influence county government policies
  • Promote child protection

Life of Dignity & Fulfillment

Support talent development among children and young persons, enabling them to achieve a life of dignity and fulfillment for themselves and their families, breaking generational cycles of poverty and creating lasting positive change.
